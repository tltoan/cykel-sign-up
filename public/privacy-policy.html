1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo
  • Profile Preferences: Style preferences, favorite eras, sizes, shopping interests
  • Store Information (for verified stores): Business name, address, license information, storefront photos
  • Communication: Messages between buyers and sellers, customer support inquiries
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, shipping addresses, payment method information (processed by Stripe)
  • Content: Item listings, photos, descriptions, reviews, and ratings

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, time spent in app, search queries
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (with permission) to show nearby stores and calculate distances
  • Technical Data: IP address, log files, crash reports, performance data

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • POS System Data: Inventory information from connected Square, Shopify, or Stripe accounts
  • Payment Processing: Transaction data from Stripe (we don't store full payment card information)
  • Social Media: Profile information if you choose to link social accounts

3. Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share your data in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 With Other Users

  • Store owners can see buyer names and shipping addresses for completed purchases
  • Your profile information is visible to stores you message
  • Reviews and ratings you leave may be publicly visible

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing and transaction data
  • Firebase: App hosting, database, and authentication services
  • Unified.to: POS system integrations for inventory sync
  • Cloud Storage: Secure storage of photos and app data
  • Push Notifications: Delivery of app notifications

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, such as:

  • Response to legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protecting rights, property, or safety of Cykle, users, or others
  • Preventing fraud or investigating suspected illegal activity

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing monitoring and security assessments
  • Secure Infrastructure: Firebase and AWS provide enterprise-grade security
  • Payment Security: Stripe handles payment processing with PCI DSS compliance

Note: While we use best practices to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account information confidential.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Management

  • Access: View and download your personal data through the app
  • Update: Modify your profile information and preferences anytime
  • Delete: Request account deletion by contacting support

5.2 Communication Preferences

  • Push Notifications: Enable/disable in your device settings
  • Email Notifications: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • SMS: Opt out of text messages by replying STOP

5.3 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format by contacting privacy@cykle.co.

6. Location Data

Cykle uses location data to enhance your experience:

  • Store Discovery: Find vintage stores near your location
  • Distance Calculations: Show how far stores and items are from you
  • Local Recommendations: Suggest relevant stores and items

Your Control: You can disable location access anytime in your device settings. Some features may be limited without location access.

7. Third-Party Services

Cykle integrates with several third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Stripe: stripe.com/privacy
  • Firebase/Google: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Unified.to: unified.to/privacy

8. Children's Privacy

Cykle is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

Cykle is based in the United States. If you use our service from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have different privacy laws than your country.

9.1 European Users (GDPR)

If you're in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities
