Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on Cykle?
Download the Cykle app from the App Store, tap "Sign Up," and follow the onboarding process. You'll need to provide an email address and set up your style preferences for personalized recommendations.
How do I know if a store is verified?
All stores on Cykle go through our verification process. Look for the "Verified Store" badge on store profiles. We verify business licenses and physical storefronts to ensure authenticity.
How do payments work on Cykle?
We use Stripe for secure payment processing. You can pay with credit/debit cards or digital wallets. Your payment information is encrypted and secure.
What if an item isn't as described?
First, contact the store directly through our messaging system. If you can't resolve the issue, contact our support team at support@cykle.co for mediation.
How do I return or exchange an item?
Return policies are set by individual stores. Check the store's return policy before purchasing, or message the store owner to discuss return options.
Can I follow my favorite stores?
Yes! Tap the "Follow" button on any store profile to get notifications when they add new items to their inventory.

Buying Guide

Getting Started as a Shopper

  • Browse Local Stores: Use the map feature to discover vintage stores near you
  • Filter by Category: Use filters like "Under $25," "Designer," or "New Arrivals" to find what you're looking for
  • Message Store Owners: Ask questions about sizing, condition, or additional photos
  • Save Favorites: Heart items you love to save them for later
  • Secure Checkout: Complete your purchase with confidence using our secure payment system

Store Owner Help

How to Become a Verified Store

  • Application: Complete our store verification form in the app
  • Documentation: Upload your business license and storefront photos
  • Review Process: Our team reviews applications within 24-48 hours
  • POS Integration: Connect your existing POS system (Square, Shopify, Stripe) for automatic inventory sync
  • Start Selling: Begin listing your vintage inventory and reaching new customers

Store Dashboard Features

  • Track active listings and sales performance
  • Manage customer messages and inquiries
  • View analytics and growth trends
  • Update store information and hours
  • Sync with your existing POS system

Account & Technical Support

Common Account Issues

  • Forgot Password: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen
  • Email Verification: Check your email for the verification link after signing up
  • Update Profile: Go to your profile tab to update personal information and preferences
  • Location Services: Enable location access to discover nearby stores
  • Push Notifications: Enable notifications to get alerts about new items from followed stores
